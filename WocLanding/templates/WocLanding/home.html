Get fresh insights into the world of Programming and Development

WEEKEND OF CODE is a joint initiative of B.Tech final and pre-final year students to provide the freshers an exposure to the world of competitive programming alongwith a glimpse of software development. The Weekend Of Code basically consists of two events:

  • CodeStart: CodeStart is a 3 hours ACM-ICPC styled onsite coding contest. The event aims at providing freshers an experience equivalent to participating in international coding event.
  •       DevJam: DevJam is 7 days online/onsite development event which aims at familiarising the freshers with the latest frameworks and technologies in IT industry. This event will allow the freshers to learn new technologies and will groom them for participating in various national and international events and thus, take the name of college to new heights.


  • Who is WOC for?
    DevJAm - B.tech 1st Years + MCA 1st Year
    CodeStart - B.tech (1st+2nd Year) and MCA 1st year
